About Our Company

Avivo is a technology company specializing in contract intelligence, document understanding, and contract management solutions. Our mission is to help businesses transform how they interpret, analyze, and manage contractual information.

We build advanced tools powered by natural language processing (NLP) and generative AI to extract actionable insights from contracts, automate manual review processes, and maintain compliance across large document ecosystems.

Our products are designed to assist teams in:
Identifying key terms, obligations, and risks
Automating repetitive contract review workflows
Reducing human error and speeding up approval cycles
Ensuring consistent compliance with internal and external policies
Integrating contract intelligence into existing business systems

At Avivo, we prioritize:

Accuracy
Our AI models are continuously fine-tuned to deliver precise and reliable results, even across complex legal language.

Privacy & Security
We implement strict data-handling standards, encryption, and access controls. Customer data is never used for model training without explicit approval.

Developer-Friendly Integrations
Our tools support seamless integration with existing systems through well-documented APIs, SDKs, and workflow connectors.

Customer-Centric Innovation
We work closely with clients to improve our products, deliver tailored solutions, and support business-critical use cases.

Avivo is committed to building trustworthy, efficient, and scalable contract intelligence technologies that empower organizations to work smarter and faster.
